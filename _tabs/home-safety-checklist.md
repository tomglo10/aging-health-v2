### Senior Home Safety Audit
Optimizing the living environment to extend "Healthspan" and prevent acute injury.

* **Lighting:** 1000+ lumens in transition areas (hallways, stairs).
* **Bathroom:** Grab bars installed into studs (not suction-cup).
* **Kitchen:** Heavy items moved to waist-height shelves.
* **Flooring:** Non-slip backing on all rugs or removal of throw rugs.
