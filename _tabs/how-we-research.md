## 🔬 Clinical Research Standards 2026
Our methodology focuses on translating complex clinical data into actionable health literacy for seniors.

### 🧪 Cytokine & Inflammatory Marker Glossary
* **TNF-α (Tumor Necrosis Factor):** A pro-inflammatory cytokine linked to joint damage and endothelial dysfunction.
* **IL-17 / IL-23:** Drivers of systemic inflammation in psoriasis.
* **IL-6:** A marker of systemic stress often linked to insulin resistance.
* **hs-CRP:** High-sensitivity test for general vascular inflammation.

> **Research Note:** We manage health as a **Vascular Bridge**. What happens in the gut or liver directly impacts the brain and heart.
