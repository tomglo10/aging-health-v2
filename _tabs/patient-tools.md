## 📋 Clinical Advocacy Toolkit
*Tools and reference matrices designed to help seniors and advocates navigate complex metabolic and vascular health decisions.*

---

### 🔍 Metabolic Mimics Lab Matrix
In geriatric care, many "chronic" symptoms are actually reversible metabolic states. Use this matrix to prepare for a differential diagnosis conversation with your specialist.

| Symptom                                 | Often Labeled As         | The Metabolic Mimic         | Required Labs                       |
| :-------------------------------------- | :----------------------- | :-------------------------- | :---------------------------------- |
| **Flapping tremors, personality shift** | Dementia / Alzheimer's   | **Hepatic Encephalopathy**  | Serum Ammonia, LFTs (Liver Panel)   |
| **Sudden slurred speech, weakness**     | TIA or Stroke            | **Hypoglycemia**            | Instant Finger-stick Glucose        |
| **Balance loss, tingling, "fog"**       | Parkinson's / Neuropathy | **Vitamin B12 Deficiency**  | Serum B12, MMA (Methylmalonic Acid) |
| **Acute confusion, hallucinations**     | Late-stage Dementia      | **Hyponatremia (Low Salt)** | BMP or CMP (Electrolytes)           |
| **Fatigue, depression, thinning hair**  | Clinical Depression      | **Hypothyroidism**          | TSH, Free T4, Free T3               |

---

### 🩸 The "Silent" Mimic: Delirium vs. Dementia
Sudden onset of confusion in seniors is frequently a **Urinary Tract Infection (UTI)**. Unlike younger adults, seniors often do not present with fever or pain. **Always demand a Urinalysis (UA)** when cognitive shifts occur rapidly.

---

### 📈 2026 Resilience Standards
When advocating for yourself or a loved one, keep these targets at the forefront of the conversation:

* **Protein Threshold ($1.2\text{--}1.6 \text{ g/kg}$):** Required to maintain transport proteins that clear metabolic waste.
* **Vascular Integrity ($<130/80 \text{ mmHg}$):** The gold standard for neuro-protection and heart safety.
* **The "Metabolic Rule":** If symptoms fluctuate significantly over 48 hours, investigate the liver and gut chemistry before assuming a progressive neurological disease.
