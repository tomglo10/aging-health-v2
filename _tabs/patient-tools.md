### Interactive Health Literacy Tools
These tools are designed to help seniors and advocates manage clinical data at home.

* **[Protein Calculator](#):** Determine your RDA based on the 1.2 – 1.6 g/kg target.
* **[Vascular Sync Tracker](#):** Log blood pressure and A1c readings.
* **[Glossary of Terms](#):** Instant translation of clinical jargon found in your 2026 labs.
* **[Medication Log](#):** Printable template for specialist appointments.
